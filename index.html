FAQ

  • What is Coco Valley: Farm Adventure?
    Coco Valley: Farm Adventure is a farming simulator set in a magical valley. Players can plant crops, raise animals, complete tasks, and explore new areas as they build their dream farm.
  • Is the game free to play?
    Yes, the game is free to download and play. In-app purchases are available for extra items, decorations, and boosts.
  • Can I play offline?
    You can play Coco Valley: Farm Adventure offline for basic activities, but certain features, like events and updates, require an internet connection.
  • How do I unlock new areas?
    New areas are unlocked by progressing through the game, completing farming tasks, and achieving specific goals.
  • Are there any time-limited events?
    Yes! Coco Valley: Farm Adventure regularly features time-limited events, special crops, and limited-edition animals that add more excitement to your farming journey.
  • Can I play with friends?
    Yes! You can connect with friends to share farm progress and participate in collaborative farming tasks and special events.
